About Farm management system

Mission: To provide technology and services to farmers,merchants and farm labourers to help them expand their business and provide them with a wider market and to improve the present farming process and to provide knowledge about recent agriculture issues.
Vision: To provide a helping hand to the farmers and farm labourer in improving their lives through the medium of technology thereby improving the agriculture sector in the bangladeshi economy.
